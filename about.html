About Us

Welcome to Ordesea Resources Limited

Geological & Mining Company

Company Profile

Ordesea Resources Limited is a Geological & Mining Company, founded in 2008, that specializes in mining and geological field consultation services across Nigeria, as well as overseas. We deal with mining at every stage, both at the local, national, and international level.

With over eleven years of experience, we have gathered a stellar list of national and international clientele. We are fully registered with the Nigerian Export Promotion Council.

Our Specialties

Geophysical Survey & Geochemical Analysis

20% of our expertise is dedicated to providing geophysical survey and geochemical analysis services.

Mechanization & Operations

20% of our expertise is focused on raising funds and setting up full-scale mining operations.

Logistics & Transportation

15% of our expertise is dedicated to the movement of minerals from the site to the point of use.

Mining Site Acquisitions & Lease Processing

10% of our expertise is involved in acquiring mining sites and processing lease agreements.

International Export and Import

35% of our expertise is focused on providing technical information regarding export and import of minerals.
